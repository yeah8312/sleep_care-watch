Implementation notes

Rules carried from Stitch

No hard 1px dividers. Use tonal shifts, gradients, and spacing for separation. Favor asymmetry and large margins over dense smartwatch dashboards. Use Manrope for hero and headline moments, Inter for utility text and data labels. Warning states should feel like a soft alarm, not a high-saturation emergency banner.
